passenger_id: id of passenger
survived: ‘’Yes’ means the passenger survived, ‘No’ means the passenger didn’t survive.
pclass: ticket class, ‘1’ for 1st class, ‘2’ for 2nd class, ‘3’ for 3rd class.
name: name of passenger, passenger title, surname of passenger
sex: sex of the passenger
age: age of the passengersib_sp: number of siblings or spouses aboard the Titanic
parch: number of parents or children aboard the Titanic
ticket: ticket number
fair: passenger fare
cabin: cabin number
embarked: ‘C’ for ’Cherbourg’, ‘Q’ for ‘Queenstown’, ‘S’ for ‘Southampton’
